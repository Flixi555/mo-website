Moritz S. Graefrath

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Political Science at the University of Notre Dame. I specialize in International Relations.

Broadly speaking, my research interests include international relations theory, great power politics, and international security. Currently, I investigate the role of intentions in international politics, sovereign debt as a foreign policy instrument, and states’ use of international conflict settlement venues.

Prior to coming to Notre Dame, I received a B.A. in Philosophy & Economics from the University of Bayreuth, Germany. I have also studied at Washington & Lee University (Lexington, VA) as an exchange student.
